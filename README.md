# Perditio

Perditio is an AI-native culture platform building systems that turn cultural moments into scalable, personalized experiences.

## What We’re Building

We design infrastructure for discovering, creating, and participating in culture through AI.

Our current focus is multi-agent systems that:
- coordinate execution across agents
- personalize experiences in real time
- support creators, communities, and cultural ecosystems

## Approach

We treat community as infrastructure.

We build systems where:
- agents operate with defined roles and ownership
- workflows are structured and observable
- human and AI collaboration scales by design

## Why It Matters

Most platforms aggregate culture.

Perditio builds systems that generate it.

## Public vs Private

We share selected components to demonstrate how AI-native systems are built.

Core infrastructure, workflows, and business logic remain private.
